Dog & Puppy Daycare

Fitting an hour-long workout for your dog into your own hectic schedule can be a challenge—but dogs that don’t get enough exercise and stimulation are more likely to bark excessively, jump up, pull on leash, snap at other dogs, and generally misbehave.

Our Day Camp dog daycare is the perfect solution. Drop off your dog knowing she’ll spend the day playing with her friends and practicing her manners. Pick her up tired, happy, and ready to doze at your feet. No more guilt, and a healthier, better-behaved dog.

  • Small, supervised groups
  • Essential downtime
  • Enrichment Items
  • Training reinforcement
  • Indoor/Outdoor play

Dog Boarding

If your dog has never been boarded before, hasn’t stayed with someone else overnight, or has had a bad boarding experience in the past, we encourage you to schedule a one-night trial stay ($52). This way, we can make sure your dog is comfortable with us while you’re away.
