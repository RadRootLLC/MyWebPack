Extended Training Program

Tail Lights Dogs trains your dog for you during an initial customized schedule, based on your dog's previous training, age, temperament, behavior habits, and tractability. Our professional trainers then maintain the improved behavior for you via attendance in our Day School, twice per week.

What are the benefits?
Lasting changes in your dog's behavior. You save time and effort when your dog is trained by a professional.

Who’s it for?
Busy families and professionals. Anyone who lacks the time or desire to train and wants relief from behavior problems.

What type of dog benefits from the Extended Training Program?
Adolescents and other dogs with high energy and drive. Dogs who are in need of structure and professional guidance. Dogs who come from working dog lines. Dogs in need of remedial training and socialization. Dogs who are candidates for competitive or therapy work.

Day School

Your dog receives at least two training sessions per day with one of our professional trainers during Tail Lights Dogs Daycare.

What are the benefits?
Good behavior is maintained through consistency. Continued training challenges and safe socialization are enriching experiences for your dog.

Who's it for?
Busy families and professionals. Anyone who lacks the time or desire to train and wants to maintain their dog's good behavior and social skills.

What type of dog benefits from Day School?
All dogs benefit from continuing education. For dogs who have completed group class, Tail Lights Dogs Day School is a great way to tune-up obedience and fine tune competition skills.

The Right Start Puppy Program

In this innovative premium program, Tail Lights Dogs trains and socializes your puppy for you during a 6 week program scheduled for your convenience. Boarding stays, with a reduced overnight rate on training days, may be combined with The Right Start.

Puppy attends Day School 3 days per week for the first 3 weeks, then 2 days per week for the second 3 weeks of the program. During this time, you may attend as many puppy group classes as you can fit into your busy schedule. The program includes three bathing experiences, by our gentle, positive trainers to help your puppy learn to like baths, nail trims, and handling.

At the conclusion of the first week, you will have a private lesson with a Tail Lights trainer to help you get started with the appropriate training equipment and good on-leash behavior. At the end of the 6 week program, another private lesson helps you integrate your puppy's new skills into real-life situations, and gets you ready for the next step. A relaxation mat and harness are included in the program.
